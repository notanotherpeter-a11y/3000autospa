FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.
What areas do you cover in Melbourne?▼
We service all metropolitan Melbourne suburbs across three zones. Inner suburbs (0–10km from CBD) have no travel fee. Middle suburbs (10–25km) have a $20 fee. Outer suburbs (25–40km) have a $35 fee.
Do I need to be home during the service?▼
Not necessarily. As long as we can access your car in the driveway, you don't need to supervise us. Many customers work from home or run errands while we detail. We'll SMS you when we're done.
What do I need to provide?▼
Just your car parked in the driveway. We bring everything — water, power, equipment, and products. No drop-off, no effort on your part.
What's your cancellation policy?▼
4+ hours notice: no charge. 2–4 hours notice: 50% fee. Under 2 hours: full charge applies. Weather cancellations are always free — we'll reschedule at no cost.
Do you use eco-friendly products?▼
Yes! We offer eco-conscious cleaning methods using biodegradable, waterless or low-water products. Just mention it when booking.
How long does each service take?▼
Express Wash: 1–2 hours. Full Detail: 2–4 hours. Ceramic Coating: 4–6 hours. These are estimates based on vehicle size.
What's included in the satisfaction guarantee?▼
If you're not 100% happy, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-service — completely free. No arguments, no drama.
